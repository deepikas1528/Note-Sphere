How to Use NoteSphere

A short guide for creating notes, lists, pins, and tasks.

  • Create notes: Add a title and content, then click Save.
  • Format ideas: Use bullet list, numbered list, task line, or template buttons while writing.
  • Organize notes: Search by keyword, sort notes, or pin important notes to find them faster.
  • Reuse notes: Click Copy to copy a note title and content to your clipboard.
  • Track tasks: Add checklist items in the Tasks section and remove finished tasks when done.
